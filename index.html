A QUICK DIVE INTO EVERY GOAL SCORED BY A BACKHAND SHOT IN 21-22 NHL SEASON

Using shot data from the 21-22 NHL Season on Moneypuck.com. I pulled every backhand shot and found the average distance for each goal, goalie freeze, and shot that created a rebound. It was a simple analysis, but can teach us a lot. For defenders and goalies, players are not a threat on their backhand until they are about 15 feet from the net, so be patient. Forwards, if you are within 15 feet of the net you can take that shot, but if not, it is more beneficial to your team if you cutback or chip it to space.
